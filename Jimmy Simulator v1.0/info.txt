--CONTROLS--
WASD to move
QE to turn/attack
tab to block
R to heal

--CHEATS--
1 to go back a level
2 to go forward a level
3 to heal
4 to remove all enemies

enable/disable cheats in the launcher
attempting to go to a level that does not exist will result in an error

--INSTRUCTIONS--
move to the right side of the screen once all enemies are defeated to progress
health packs will appear on certain levels

non-horror mode disables the jumpscares on level 10
